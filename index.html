A tribute to Jimmy Sakurai's

OBSESSION

IF YOU'VE NEVER HEARD OF AKIO SAKURAI...

IT'S BECAUSE THAT NAME DOESN'T EXIST.

FOR THE PAST 30 YEARS HE'S BEEN JIMMY SAKURAI.

THE MOST THOROUGH IMITATOR OF JIMMY PAGE.

AKIO WAS BORN IN JAPAN.

BUT WAS REBORN AFTER LISTENING TO LED ZEPPELIN.

FROM THEN ONWARDS, HE ASPIRED TO PERFORM EXACTLY LIKE THE BAND.

ON A MICROSCOPICAL LEVEL.

LIKE AN OBSESSED MONK, FULLFILLING HIS DUTY.

MANY WOULD SAY HE'D GONE TOO FAR.

"NOT FAR ENOUGH", HE'D REPLY.
